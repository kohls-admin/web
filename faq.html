How to add Kohl's Admin Infinite to my game?

  1. Go to the Kohl's Admin Infinite model page on Roblox: Click here.
  2. In Roblox Studio, open your game.
  3. Insert the Kohl's Admin Infinite model into your game by placing it in the Workspace or ServerScriptService.
  4. Publish your game to Roblox.
  5. To verify that Kohl's Admin Infinite is properly installed, visit your game directly from the Roblox website. Note that testing from Roblox Studio may not work correctly.
  6. Be cautious and ensure that you are using the correct model. There are many malicious and illegitimate copies of Kohl's Admin Infinite designed to trick users into adding a backdoor to their game. Always use the official model linked above.

Is Kohl's Admin Infinite broken right now?

Only if it does not work in the official testing place. If you are experiencing issues, please contact us in the general chat or through direct messages!

What is Infinite Studios' relationship with Kohl's Admin Infinite?

Infinite Studios is a development group made by Kohl. Kohl's Admin Infinite is made solely by Kohl under the Infinite branding.

Can Kohl's Admin Infinite developers or Infinite Studios members use commands in my game?

They can use the purchasable VIP commands, if they are not disabled.
